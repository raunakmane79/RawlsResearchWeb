Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Find answers to common questions about the Rawls Faculty Publications Database.
Q1
What is the purpose of this page?
▾
To store in an organized manner, peer-reviewed, research-categorized 5* and 5-ranked publications attributed to Rawls authors at any point in their careers. In addition, the information can be searched, filtered, and exported.
Q2
My article, which meets the criteria (5* or 5-ranked, research-categorized article), has been accepted for publication. What information do I need to provide to add it to the database?
▾
Please click on the "New Submission" button to fill out a form with all the necessary information, submit it, and the information will be added to the database within 72 hours.
Q3
What is the source of the entries stored in this page?
▾
The source is a curated mix of data coming from Academic Analytics, Faculty Success, and author-submitted notifications sent to the Rawls Research unit whenever a new 5* or 5 publication by one or more Rawls authors gets accepted for publication.
Q4
One of the research articles already listed has an error, is not actually research (editorial, commentary, etc.), or is missing additional information. How do I get it updated?
▾
Please click on the “Request Modification” button to fill out a form with all the necessary updates and submit it. The database will be updated within 72 hours.
Q5
I am a new Faculty at Rawls, and I would like to add my past publications that meet the criteria to the database.
▾
Please click on the "New Submission" button to fill out a form with all the necessary information, submit it, and the page will be updated within 72 hours. If you have more than 3 articles published prior to your appointment at Rawls, you can also send us a single spreadsheet containing all your publications. Please ensure all form questions are addressed for each article. Once submitted, the page will be updated within 5 business days.
Q6
I will be leaving Rawls; will my publication information remain in this database?
▾
Yes, we will keep this information.
If you have additional questions, please reach out to laura.bilbao@ttu.edu.
